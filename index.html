The house

Highland hospitality,
poured the Amman way.

BAN Coffee House borrows its soul from the old Scottish coffee houses — those snug, wood-warmed rooms where conversation lingers and nobody hurries you out the door. We've carried that feeling to Um Uthaina, set just opposite the Ministry of Transport, beside Sayf.

The coffee is specialty through and through: beans dialled in daily, espresso pulled with care, filter brewed bright and clean. Alongside it, a counter of fresh-baked croissants and bakery — flaky, buttery, made for dunking. It's become a quiet favourite in the neighbourhood, and we'd love for it to become one of yours.
